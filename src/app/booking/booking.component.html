WELCOME TO THE CHAMPAGNE CASTLE HOTEL

The Beauty of The champagne-castle-view

As a guest of Champagne Castle Hotel you have access to daily Guided Walks, Lawn Bowls, Tennis, Badminton, the Animal Farm and Bird Aviary completely free of charge. Our location at the top of the Champagne Valley ensures a diverse variety of activities, places of interest and a multitude of things to do and explore, all within a short distance. TripAdvisor’s top 2 rated activities in the area (The Drakensberg Boys Choir, and Falcon Ridge Raptor Show) are within 5km of the Hotel.

The resort offers a variety of superb, fully serviced accommodation. The 152 hotel bedrooms are made up of twin rooms, family rooms, and various suites, all with well-appointed bedrooms and either mountain views or garden views. A further 91 chalets on the estate are used for hotel accommodation subject to availability. The hotel has wheelchair access throughout and all bedrooms are linked to the main hotel buildings by way of thatch covered walkways.

If guests prefer good view they should be able to select the best type of room. Providing the guest with more options to upgrade the room where they can choose to stay near the beach or at the beach.

We appointed Johannesburg based Pulp Films to put two promotional videos together of Champagne Castle Hotel. We wanted a video that would capture both the Hotel and our Self Catering accommodation. We wanted people to see the beauty of the Drakensberg mountains that we’re nestled up against, and the wide open invitation to explore them. We definitely wanted past, current and future guests to get a feel for the essence of what they would experience during their stay at one of South Africa’s Premier Resorts
